About Me

Hi, I'm Olivia!

I got my B.M.E. in Music Education from the University of North Texas in 2021. My focus was in elementary school, where I lived my passion teaching music literacy to young children through play and song.

Although I loved teaching in public school, I feel called to bring these same musical opportunities to young children at home! I believe each child deserves to understand and connect to music on a deeper level. The personal relationship I develop with my private students is unlike anything I experienced in the classroom, and I'm excited to get to know you!
