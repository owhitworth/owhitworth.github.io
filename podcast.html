FAQ

Can my older kids take the class too?

Sure! Even though this program is designed for k-3, get your whole family involved in the fun! Music is better together.

How much do I need to practice?

Just 15-20 minutes of music a day is plenty to develop a strong musical foundation. If you have enough time to watch a youtube video, you've got enough time to learn music!
